📘 Frequently Asked Questions

To update your email address:

  1. Navigate to the 'Account' section in the sidebar
  2. Click on 'Edit Profile'
  3. Update your email address in the form
  4. Click 'Save Changes'
  5. Verify your new email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

Note: You'll need to verify your new email before the change takes effect.

Yes! You can connect with classmates through the Chat feature:

  • Access the 'Chat' section from the sidebar
  • Search for classmates by name or browse course groups
  • Start a direct message or join a course discussion group

All students enrolled in the same course can be found in the course chat group.

If your newly enrolled course isn't appearing:

  • Try refreshing the page (press F5 or the refresh button)
  • Log out and log back in to reset your session
  • Check if the course start date has arrived
  • Verify your enrollment status under 'Account' → 'My Enrollments'

If the course still doesn't appear, contact your instructor or support team at support@educonnect.com.

EduConnect offers multiple ways to monitor your progress:

  • View your overall GPA in the Dashboard
  • Check detailed course grades in the 'Grades' section
  • Review assignment progress in each course page
  • Download progress reports from the 'Grades' section

You can also enable weekly progress email notifications in your account settings.

To change your password:

  1. Go to 'Account' in the sidebar
  2. Select 'Security Settings'
  3. Click 'Change Password'
  4. Enter your current password and your new password twice
  5. Click 'Update Password'

For security reasons, you'll be logged out and need to log in with your new password.

To change your profile picture:

  1. Navigate to 'Account'
  2. Click on 'Edit Profile'
  3. Click on the profile picture icon or current photo
  4. Select 'Upload New Photo'
  5. Choose an image (JPG, PNG, max 5MB)
  6. Crop if needed, then click 'Save'

Your profile picture will be visible to instructors and classmates.

To upload an assignment:

  1. Go to 'Courses' and select your course
  2. Navigate to the 'Assignments' tab
  3. Find the assignment and click 'Submit'
  4. Click 'Upload Files' or drag files to the upload area
  5. Select your file(s) (supported formats: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, ZIP, max 50MB)
  6. Add any comments for your instructor (optional)
  7. Click 'Submit Assignment'

You'll receive a confirmation email once your submission is complete.

Yes, you can withdraw from a course before the withdrawal deadline:

  1. Go to 'Courses' in the sidebar
  2. Find the course you want to withdraw from
  3. Click the three-dot menu and select 'Course Options'
  4. Choose 'Withdraw from Course'
  5. Confirm your decision

Important: Check your institution's withdrawal policies for deadlines and potential academic/financial impacts.

For optimal performance, we recommend:

  • Browsers: Chrome 89+, Firefox 86+, Safari 14+, Edge 89+
  • Operating Systems: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14+, Chrome OS, Ubuntu 18.04+
  • Mobile: iOS 13+ or Android 10+
  • Internet: Broadband connection (1+ Mbps)
  • Storage: At least 1GB free space for downloaded materials
  • Additional: JavaScript enabled, cookies allowed

For virtual classrooms and video content, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer.

If you're experiencing technical issues, clearing your browser cache often helps:

Chrome:

  1. Click the three dots in the top-right corner
  2. Select 'More tools' → 'Clear browsing data'
  3. Select 'Cached images and files'
  4. Click 'Clear data'

Firefox:

  1. Click the menu button (three lines) in the top-right
  2. Select 'Settings' → 'Privacy & Security'
  3. In 'Cookies and Site Data', click 'Clear Data'
  4. Select 'Cached Web Content' and click 'Clear'

After clearing cache, refresh the page and try again.

🔧 Common Troubleshooting

Page Won't Load

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Clear browser cache and cookies
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Disable browser extensions

Can't Upload Files

  1. Ensure file is under size limit (50MB)
  2. Check file format is supported
  3. Try renaming file (avoid special characters)
  4. Use Chrome or Firefox for uploads

Video Playback Issues

  1. Update your browser to latest version
  2. Check internet speed (min 1Mbps)
  3. Lower video quality in settings
  4. Try watching during off-peak hours

Login Problems

  1. Reset password via "Forgot Password"
  2. Check Caps Lock is off
  3. Clear browser cookies
  4. Contact support if account is locked
